This website is dedicated to the life and legacy of Bruce Lee, the martial arts legend and film star.

Bruce Lee

Bruce Lee, born as Lee Jun-fan, was a Hong Kong-American actor, director, martial artist, and philosopher. He is widely considered one of the most influential martial artists of all time and a pop culture icon of the 20th century. He was the founder of Jeet Kune Do, a hybrid martial arts philosophy drawing from different combat disciplines. He is credited with promoting Hong Kong action cinema and helping to change the way Chinese people were presented in American films.

His Philosophy

Bruce Lee's philosophy often mirrored his fighting beliefs, though he was quick to claim that his martial arts were solely a metaphor for such teachings. He believed that any knowledge ultimately led to self-knowledge, and said that his chosen method of self-expression was martial arts. His philosophy can be summarized using his own words: “having no method as method; having no limit as limit.".

His Legacy

Bruce Lee's star continues to shine brightly in the world of martial arts and film. His movies, philosophy, and training methods continue to inspire millions of people around the world. He bridged the gap between East and West and his influence is still felt today in the fields of martial arts, film, and popular culture.
